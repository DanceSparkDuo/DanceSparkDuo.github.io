Welcome to the ultimate destination for Salsa, Bachata, and Kizomba workshops! Whether you're a beginner looking to step into the vibrant world of Latin dance or an experienced dancer aiming to refine your skills, our workshops offer something for everyone.

Why Choose Our Workshops?

  • Expert Instructors: Learn from experienced professionals who are passionate about sharing their love for dance.
  • Comprehensive Curriculum: Our workshops cover everything from basic steps to advanced techniques, ensuring a well-rounded learning experience.
  • Fun and Engaging: Dance is not just about learning steps; it's about having fun, meeting new people, and expressing yourself.

Workshops and Private Lessons Available

We offer group workshops that are perfect for social dancers who enjoy learning in a community setting. If you prefer personalized attention and a customized learning pace, our private lessons are ideal for you.

Book a Workshop or Private Lesson

Ready to dance? Email us at hendrikjekel@gmail.com to schedule a workshop or to get a quotation for private lessons. Whether you're organizing an event, planning a special occasion, or just want to learn for fun, we're here to help you dance your heart out.
