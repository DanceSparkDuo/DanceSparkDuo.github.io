About Us

Welcome to our world of dance, where movement, passion, and teaching converge. We are Henk Jekel and Elies van Hees, a duo dedicated to spreading the joy of salsa, bachata, and kizomba through our engaging workshops. Our journey is not just about dance; it's about community, learning, and sharing moments of joy.

July 05, 1997 - Now

Henk Jekel

By day, I'm a robotics engineer, intricately weaving the dance of machines in harmony. My life is a testament to the belief that precision and creativity can coexist, guiding my approach to dance. As an aspiring sports coach and a teaching assistant at the University, I've learned the art of breaking down complex movements into manageable steps, making dance accessible to everyone, regardless of their background or experience. Dance, for me, is not just a hobby; it's a way to connect, communicate, and celebrate life beyond words. My analytical mind from robotics complements my teaching methods, ensuring a unique, structured, yet profoundly enjoyable learning experience in our workshops.
